Air Fryer Garlic Green Beans

These garlic green beans are so simple.

This side dish comes together in a flash while you prepare the rest of your dinner thanks to the air fryer.

INGREDIENT

  • 12 ounces fresh green beans, ends trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

STEPS

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place green beans in a bowl. Add olive oil, garlic, seasoned salt, and ground black pepper. Toss to coat.
  3. Place green beans in the basket of the air fryer. Cook until crisp-tender, about 10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through cooking time.
